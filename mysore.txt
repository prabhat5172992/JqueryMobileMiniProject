Mysore is a tourism hot spot within the state of Karnataka and also acts as a base for other tourist places in the vicinity of the city. 
The city receives the maximum number of tourists during the period of the Dasara festival when festivities take place for a period of 10 days. 
One of the most visited monuments in India, the Ambavilas Palace (also known as Mysore Palace) is the center of the Dasara festivities.
