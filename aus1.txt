Regarded as a 20th century architectural masterpiece, the Sydney Opera House was designed and built by architect, Jorn Utzon, 
to reflect the image of a huge sailing ship. It houses multiple venues that together host more than 1,500 performances each year. 
Surrounded by the beautiful scenery of the Sydney Harbour and the Royal Botanic Gardens, 
the famous Opera House in Sydney is one of Australia's most famous landmarks.
