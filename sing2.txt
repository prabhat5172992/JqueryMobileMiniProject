Resorts World Sentosa is a Singaporean destination unto itself. 
Located on an island off Singapore's south coast, the property features hotels, restaurants, a casino, theme parks, in short, something for everyone no matter how old they are. 
With this location, attractions obviously center around the sea: Marine Life Park, Dolphin Island, a water park and an aquarium. 
Other attractions include Universal Studios Singapore and nightly entertainment. 
The casino offers a variety of table games sand 2,400 slot machines; be forewarned, it does enforce a dress code. 
The resort offers more than 60 dining options for hungry guests.
