Jaipur is also popularly known as the Pink City, is the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan. 
Jaipur is a very famous tourist and education destination in India. Lots of people flock to Jaipur 
to view the various forts and monuments in Jaipur which reflect its glorious past. 
Tourism is a significant part of Jaipur's economy. Some of the world's best hotels are located here.
